Hamza

By being raised in a military family, Hamza had the opportunity to travel to over 13 countries before the age of 15. However, that privilege came with a sense of responsibility to share all the diverse cultures, perspectives and people that he has been blessed to see. Most importantly, he learned how the STORY behind everything drives a message.

With a burning desire for business strategy and a passion for creative arts, Hamza found the perfect way to bridge the gap between the two with ThriveOn Media.

Jasmine

Growing up with a natural talent for the arts, and an eagerness to think outside of the box, Jasmine has set out to become a powerhouse in video production and creative storytelling. A Boston native turned Austin TX local, she quickly made a name for herself amongst the creative community and has been working alongside brands across the country producing promotional video and photography work. Her humble beginnings include running a YouTube vlog at the age of 15 and blindly shooting entire travel vlogs using only a GoPro with no screen. She is no stranger to the struggle and as one of the Creative Directors here at Thrive she is always up for a creative challenge.

Jon

Throughout his journey, the constants in Jon’s life have been


1. A group of uplifting people

2. A positive mindset

3. The story that unfolds from the simple act of saying “yes” to opportunities

4. A camera to capture moments along the way


Any steps, whether big or small, planned or spontaneous, right or wrong, calculated or mindless, are all equally important because every action you take gets you somewhere and teaches you something. Jon’s creative vision stems from his constant focus on inspiring action.
